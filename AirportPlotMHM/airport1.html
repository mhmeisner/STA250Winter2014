Airline Data by Airport

Click on the red point corresponding to each airport to see data from that particular airport. Use the radio button above the plot to toggle between:
1. Average number of daily departures at the airport by the 11 largest domestic air carriers.
2. Distributions of arrival delay times for the airlines serving that airport.

Note: to keep down the computational burden, only data from August of 2012 was used to make these graphs!
